# Insecurity work is dangerous because it is frictionless 

The danger of insecurity work is not its speed but its lack of friction.
Deep work requires a ramp-up cost and a deliberate commitment to enter,
which gates compulsive repetition naturally. Insecurity work bypasses all
of that — it can be repeated on impulse without preparation or cost. Speed
is the symptom; frictionlessness is the mechanism.
