# The antidote to outcome-driven anxiety is treating the process as the destination

The dissonance between aspiration and present reality tempts you to live in
the future — imagining failures, envying competitors, measuring yourself
against outcomes not yet reached. The remedy is to treat the process as the
destination rather than the means. The path is made by walking it, not by
studying it from a distance.
