# Industry norms pull a business toward conformity

The best entrepreneurs orient their business around self-expression because
industry norms will shape a business to resemble the rest. This self-expression
means injecting what is distinctly yours into the work, down to the metrics
you choose to measure, which should reflect the things you value.
